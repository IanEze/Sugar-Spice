F.A.Q

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

Our cancellation and refund policy allows for a full refund if the cancellation is made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled service. Cancellations made within 24 hours are subject to a 50% refund. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.


Do you have a loyalty or rewards program?

Yes, we value our loyal customers! We have an exciting Loyalty Program that allows you to earn delicious rewards with every purchase. For each transaction, you'll accumulate points that can be redeemed for exclusive discounts, free treats, and special offers.


Do you offer sugar-free or low-sugar options?

Yes, we offer sugar-free and low-sugar options for some of our products. Our bakers carefully craft these treats to provide delicious alternatives for those with dietary preferences or restrictions. Please check our menu or inquire at the counter for the current selection.


Can I customize a cake for a special occasion?

: Certainly! We love creating custom cakes for special occasions. Visit our "Custom Orders" page for more information or contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements.


Do you provide nutritional information for your products?

Yes, we understand the importance of nutritional information. You can find basic nutritional details on our website, and for specific inquiries, feel free to contact us.


Can I request a sample before placing a large order?

Unfortunately, we do not offer samples. However, you can check our reviews and testimonials to get a sense of the quality and taste of our products.
